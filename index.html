Understanding Customer Purchase Behavior

Analyzing customer purchase behavior helps businesses uncover patterns and trends in consumer decision-making. By understanding how and why customers make purchases, businesses can refine their marketing strategies, optimize product offerings, and increase customer satisfaction. This analysis considers various factors such as psychological, social, and economic influences that shape consumer choices.
